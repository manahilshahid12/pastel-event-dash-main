# Bloom: AI-Powered Unified Event, Content, and Community Management Platform

## Project Overview

Bloom is a unified internal platform designed to serve as a single source of truth for all company events, content planning, team collaboration, guest management, and community engagement. It consolidates event operations, content creation, and audience management into one connected system, replacing the need to manage these functions across multiple separate tools.

## Core Vision

Build an intelligent operating system that connects events, content, and community management while reducing manual work and keeping all company knowledge organized in one place.

---

## Key Components

### 1. Event Management System

**Shared Dashboard & Workspace**
- Every team member can create and manage events from a centralized dashboard
- All events are visible to the entire team in one unified workspace
- Complete organizational memory of past and upcoming events

**Structured Event Information**
- Event details and descriptions
- Dates and timelines
- Venue information
- Agenda and schedule
- Catering requirements
- Panelists and speakers
- Task assignments and responsibilities
- Deadlines and reminders
- Event preparation checklists

**Team Collaboration**
- All team members can contribute information
- Upload and share materials
- Real-time collaboration around event planning and execution

### 2. Event Media and Asset Management

- Centralized upload and organization of event-related media
- Support for: photos, videos, documents, creative assets, and notes
- Shared media library accessible to all team members
- Organized media collection per event
- Historical archive of all events and their associated assets

### 3. Content Management Calendar

**Planning and Scheduling**
- Dedicated calendar for social media content planning
- Schedule posts and define publishing dates
- Plan content themes and campaigns
- Assign ownership and track content status
- Organize collaborations

**Visibility**
- Clear overview of what content is being published
- When content will be published
- Which platforms content targets

### 4. Content Creation Workspace

**Content Drafting & Generation**
- Draft social media posts
- Generate content ideas
- Create captions and announcements
- Prepare community updates

**Content Adaptation**
- Repurpose event content into multiple formats
- Adapt content for different social media platforms
- Transform event information and media into ready-to-publish content

### 5. Guest and Community Management System

**Centralized Guest Database**
- Automatically grows and updates based on event participation
- Stores comprehensive guest information:
  - Name and email address
  - Event attendance history
  - Previous interactions
  - Participation details
- Long-term community database for organizational memory

**Community Intelligence**
- View who attended previous events
- Track attendance frequency
- Identify guest interests and preferences
- Build community insights over time

### 6. Automated Invitations and Outreach

**Email Integration**
- Connect with email systems for automated communication
- Generic email template system with automatic personalization

**Outreach Capabilities**
- Send event invitations to previous attendees
- Community updates and announcements
- Follow-ups and engagement campaigns
- Re-engage existing community without manual list creation

### 7. Event Analytics and Intelligence

**Integration & Tracking**
- Integrate with event tools (e.g., Luma) to track:
  - Registration numbers
  - Attendance counts
  - Attendance rates
  - Participant engagement metrics
  - Event performance data

**Post-Event Intelligence**
- Automatically generate event summaries
- Attendance statistics and success indicators
- Key insights and important outcomes
- Select and highlight relevant event media
- Generate ready-to-share post-event updates

### 8. Community and Social Media Automation

**Automated Content Generation**
After each event, automatically generate:
- Internal community updates
- Slack announcements
- Social media drafts
- LinkedIn-ready posts

**Content Includes**
- Event summary
- Key highlights
- Selected event images
- Notable moments

**Review & Publish Workflow**
- Team can review and edit all auto-generated content before publishing

## Goals

1. **Centralize Operations**: Create one workspace for events, content, and community management
2. **Enable Collaboration**: Allow seamless teamwork across all company functions
3. **Build Organizational Memory**: Maintain a comprehensive archive of all events, content, and guest interactions
4. **Automate Workflows**: Reduce manual work through intelligent automation
5. **Improve Engagement**: Re-engage community and maximize event impact through data-driven insights
6. **Maximize Efficiency**: Eliminate tool switching and data silos
